Can I Get a Break?

I am annoyed. It’s never my mind which is behind my disinterest in time. Time keeps moving and I manage it reasonably well. However, my body is indefinitely trapped in a well. There’s a disconnect between my mind and body. And both are slowly failing at the loss of the other. The A’s the Bs and the Cs bring fourth to reality the extent to which I would be considered “lazy.” Yet time is what saves me. Its march to eternity fills me, and all, with uncertainty. Uncertainty with which my mind and being have come to peace. So please spare me my “oh-so-humble” community; my internal screams caused by thee already deafen my responsibilities. Y’all already mute me and my body can’t plea from fear of humility. My mind envies all that is a dream filling my body with anxiety that paralyzes me. While my thoughts disassociate, my body elates at the idea of a date he can break away; free.

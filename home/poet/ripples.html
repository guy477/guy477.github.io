Ripples.txt Skipping Stones
A blur as it glides above the water, high;
gray and small: like a distant bird in the sky.
Angled up - hopeful - with a heart so soulful,
though a face stone cold, the force down is two fold.
with a slight splash and luck from hope of the past,
a bounce makes the flight last - save ripples so vast.

A little lower the bird now flies
and the energy slowly subsides.
Looking up with a sigh, heavy... full...
the tug morphs into a steady pull.
A vision once so vast is now halved
by a smooth surface mirrored like glass.

Time suddenly just whisks by
and the surface magnifies
the fast falling gray oval;
everything is now focal.
Moments go by way too fast
forcing 'meaning' to get passed.

With a heave and sigh
and ripples nearby,
all that is hopeful
turns anecdotal
of how one's downcast
is being steadfast.

No outcry,
just a sky
blue and gold
to behold.
With the last
thoughts amassed...
